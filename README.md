## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a digital business card?
A digital business card is an online version of your traditional business card that allows you to share your contact information instantly via QR code, NFC, or link.

### Do recipients need to download an app?
No. Recipients can view your digital card directly in their browser.

### Can I customize my card?
Yes. You can customize templates, colors, branding, and QR codes.

### How do analytics work?
Track views, shares, engagement metrics, and follow-up performance directly from your dashboard.

### Can I create multiple cards?
Yes. Premium and Enterprise plans allow unlimited digital cards.

### What if I change my contact information?
Updates are reflected instantly across all shared cards.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. DigiCard uses advanced security practices to protect your information.

### Can I integrate with my CRM?
Yes. Premium and Enterprise plans support integrations and API access.
